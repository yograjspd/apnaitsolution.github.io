FAQ’S

will i get tenants through this platform?

Yes, you will receive bookings through LiveBuy. If we don't bring you a booking within 100 days, we will pay you a penalty.

how exactly will livebuy help me manage my PG?

You will get a dashboard app through which you can: 1. See all your analytics; everything will be updated automatically. 2. Manage your business. 3. Onboard tenants directly through e-KYC, rental agreement, and online check-in. 4. Avail PG managing services such as a chef, staff, etc., through the app. 5. Utilize complaints management software. 6. Access 24-hour live customer support.

the aggregator fee seems too much. is the service worth the cost?

The fee is still significantly less than what a broker charges, and you receive software, services, tenants, management, and support.

is this a scam? can i actually rent out an apartment from this website?

No, Livebuy is a government-registered and recognized Startup India company. Yes, for the first time in India, you can directly rent out an apartment online.

what is the process of renting an apartment from here?

1. Book up to 5 visits at a time. 2. A Visit Guide will accompany you during your visit. 3. If you don't like any, finalize the home. If you didn't like any place at all, you can choose additional properties to visit as well. 4. Finalize and move in.

do i have to give brokerage if i rent a place from here?

No, you won't be charged any brokerage. Instead, you will be charge a minimal convenience fee that usually is just ₹2,500 only after you decide to move in to a place.
